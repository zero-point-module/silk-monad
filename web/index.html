How a Quest Resolves

1

Hide & Commit

The Quest Master places a secret item in a chest and commits keccak256(answer) on-chain.

2

Post the Bounty

A QuestCreated event escrows native MON in the QuestFactory contract.

3

The Race

Aria and Kai explore Minecraft, open the chest, and read the secret item.

4

First Solver Wins

The first correct claim() emits QuestSolved and the contract pays the winner atomically.
